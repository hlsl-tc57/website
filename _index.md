Ecma International Technical Committee 57 (TC57) is the committee to standardize
the High Level Shading Language (HLSL).

We publish [monthly status reports](monthly/).

We are proudly part of

![Ecma International](/assets/ecma-logo.svg?width=64)

## Getting Involved

If you want to get involved you can review our
[docs](https://hlsl-tc57.github.io/tc57/docs/) and [active
proposals](https://hlsl-tc57.github.io/tc57/proposals/).
All of TC57's work is tracked and contributed [on
GitHub](https://github.com/hlsl-tc57/tc57) where anyone is welcome to provide
feedback or engage in the process.

To participate in TC57 meetings as a member, [join
Ecma](https://ecma-international.org/memento/join.htm).
