the HLSL 202x language definition which will form the basis of the initial
specification that TC57 produces.

the committee to operate smoothly.

## Summary of the 2026-04-14 Meeting

A call went out for volunteers to serve as specification editors. Chris Bieneman
has voluteered to act in this role, but is seeking participation from other Ecma
members.

The committee approved allowing the chairs and editors to merge pull requests on
non-normative changes within GitHub. This will include committee documentation
and website content.

The committee reviewed and approved PRs introducing new specification clauses
for the Terms and Definitions and Conformance clauses.

## Summary of the 2026-04-28 Meeting

### New Proposal discussions

#### Conforming Literals (#15)

The committee reached consensus that warning-related language should be removed
from the proposal. Generally the committee felt that specific implementation
behavior should either be left out of the specification entirely or captured in
an informative annex.

The committee agreed with the direction of the proposal in principle, but it
needs work before inclusion in the specification.

It has been agreed to merge this as **Under Consideration**.

#### Overload Resolution (#19)

This algorithm would provide deterministic scoring on overloads, building on top
of and beyond C++.

The committee reached consensus to record this proposal as **Under
Consideration**.
